Tarboard matches your CV and research topics against U.S. funding opportunities. Federal opportunities (NIH, NSF, DOD/CDMRP, ARPA-H) are searched live from the official Grants.gov API. Foundation, society & industry programs come from a curated database plus the Johns Hopkins Research Development cancer/oncology opportunity lists. Always verify current deadlines, amounts & eligibility on the funder's own page before applying — figures shown are best-effort and change frequently. Not affiliated with any funder. Data snapshot compiled 2026.

How it works: a deterministic engine decides eligibility (rules — free, auditable, can't hallucinate a deadline or amount); Claude reads your CV, re-ranks matches with reasons, and drafts fit briefs. For the example profiles, Claude's per-grant re-ranking is precomputed & cached (real reasoning) so the demo is instant and works offline — upload your own CV to run it live (~15–25¢/session). The moat is the two-stage design: a 165-verdict rule-derived eligibility corpus re-applied to your profile, with Claude's judgment on top. Connect Claude with the ⚙ button. Built with Claude Code.
