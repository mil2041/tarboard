Computational oncology and cancer genomics. Builds machine-learning methods on
single-cell and multi-omic data to explain therapy resistance and to stratify

- T-cell lymphoma genomics. Developed end-to-end pipelines for WES/WGS, bulk and

- Multi-omics integration. Methods for joint embedding of transcriptomic, epigenomic,
  and spatial assays, including spatial metabolomics (MALDI-MSI) and multiplexed
  imaging, to map immune architecture onto molecular subtype.

- Immunotherapy resistance. Correlative analyses from investigator-initiated trials
  characterizing anti-KLRG1 and checkpoint-blockade response in T/NK-cell malignancies.
