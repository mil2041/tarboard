Grantboard matches your CV and research topics against U.S. funding opportunities. Federal opportunities (NIH, NSF, DOD/CDMRP, ARPA-H) are searched live from the official Grants.gov API. Foundation, society & industry programs come from a curated database plus the Johns Hopkins Research Development cancer/oncology opportunity lists. Always verify current deadlines, amounts & eligibility on the funder's own page before applying — figures shown are best-effort and change frequently. Not affiliated with any funder. Data snapshot compiled 2026.

How it works: a deterministic engine decides eligibility (rules — auditable, never hallucinated); Claude reads your CV, re-ranks matches with reasons, and drafts fit briefs. Connect Claude with the ⚙ button. Built with Claude Code.
